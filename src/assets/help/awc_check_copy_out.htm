Check Out/Copy Out

 Top  Previous  Next

You can copy out or check out documents by selecting one or more documents in the results area and selecting the ribbon button for the desired command. The dialog will display the documents you selected.

 

Work Area

The default destination will be the Work Area you currently have active, but you may select a different one from the dropdown list. If you had no active Work Area, the cell will be empty and the OK button disabled until a Work Area is selected.  Selecting OK completes the Check Out or Copy Out operation and the document is placed in your Work Area.
