The Inbox selection provides a display of your Inbox. In this pane, selecting any leaf inside your Inbox will display the related documents, such as documents that are assigned to you, checked out by you, or awaiting your review.

 

You can also view other users' Inbox's in the Adept Web Client by using the Inbox Membership command available in the Adept Desktop Client and adding other user's Inbox's to your Inbox membership.

 

Selecting the three horizontal lines (hamburger) on the right side of the Inbox title bar provides a quick way to navigate to other panes.
